Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my pet's first appointment?

For your pet's first visit, please bring any previous medical records, current medications, and a list of any concerns or questions you may have.

How often should my pet have a check-up?

We recommend annual check-ups for adult pets and bi-annual check-ups for senior pets. However, the frequency may vary based on your pet's individual needs.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we offer emergency services during our operating hours. Please call ahead to ensure that our team is prepared for your pet's urgent needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including cash, credit cards, and mobile payment options. Please check with our front desk for more details.

Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment by calling our office at least 24 hours in advance. This allows us to accommodate other clients in need of care.
